About Securetek Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Securetek Solutions Private Limited specializes in protection of safety and security equipment. Our management team has over 20 years of experience in the Fire Safety & Security industry.

We design, install and maintain Fire Alarm Systems, Fire Protection Systems and Gas Suppression Systems for small offices, large office complexes, malls, industries and critical areas like server rooms — all certified to industry Codes of Practice and current regulations.

With a client retention rate of over 95%, we are trusted for delivering cost-effective, high-quality solutions tailored to individual site needs.

Using the latest technology, we provide CCTV, Intruder Alarm, Access Control and integrated security solutions. Our approach minimises risks by combining best design practices with industry standards.

Our Commitment: Reliable service, compliant installations and complete protection for people, property and critical assets.
